JUST-AI

Thank you for completing the JUST AI survey. Using the information you have shared, we have created visual representations of your research interests and perspective in the field of AI and data ethics.

We hope these perspectives will show different ways to connect, and challenge representations that consolidate power and influence. Striving for alternative means of engaging with networks, we have designed these prototypes to model transformed potentials for networking. You can find and share your own unique position and find other researchers with similar or different perspectives.

Your survey fingerprint

This is a visualization of your own perspective. Inspired by rhizome structures, each branch offers a unique insight into your research, expertise and practices.

The colour and variations adds to the complexity of these answers to situate your own unique contribution to the field. You can share your personal fingerprint with others via social media.

Part of a whole

Here is a different visualization of your perspective among other responses who have completed the survey. Rather than viewing these visualizations as static networks showing links between nodes, let’s interpret your response as part of a social process that defines the research we conduct in the field of data and AI ethics. By sharing your perspective with others, your position in this network will change in relation to other perspectives.

How did people respond to the survey thus far? Do many respond with the same answer or are they spread out widely? For each question, there is a wheel of possible answers. Each choice is recorded and added to the bars.

The placement of the wheels indicate what type of question it is.

The distance between the wheels indicate questions we think relate to each other

Each WHEEL represent one question and its possible answers

Each STACK on the wheels represent a possible answer
